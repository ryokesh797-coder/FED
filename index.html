God

Basic Information

Name: God

Nature: Eternal, Almighty

Role: Creator of the Universe

Existence: Eternal (No beginning, no end)

Attributes

  • All-powerful (Omnipotent)
  • All-knowing (Omniscient)
  • Present everywhere (Omnipresent)
  • Merciful
  • Just

Beliefs

God is believed to be the creator and sustainer of all life, worshipped across many religions and cultures.
